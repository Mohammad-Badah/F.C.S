The Solution

Create a wireless system with automated self-learning fly devices
and smart interactive wristwatches connected to a station for
early and rapidly detect and control the problems such as fire, through
advanced sensors and devices, all connected to an independent competent authority
trained to work with our special devices.

System Devices

Our System Devices will guarantee Forest safety

The Drones

Every connected system will contain a certain number of drones which will depend on the forest area, controlled by the automated self-learning station.

The Stations

The station will monitor the status of the drones, and it contains the drone wireless chargers.

Smart Wristwatches

•Forest entry Wristwatches
•In case of emergency they can press a button on the wristwatch to call the nearest drone.

Health impact

Globally: Pollution causes dangerous and deadly diseases to humans, to reduce pollution in cities, factories and cars, we must work to protect trees to reduce CO2 and O2 production by taking care of them with sophisticated methods.

Features

Our System is so efficient in a low budget !

Fire detection

Early and rapid detection of the forest problems such as fire, through advanced sensors and devices.

Fire Control

The whole world is currently heading to find environmentally friendly ways to extinguish the fire, one of these methods is using low sound frequencies to separate the oxygen from the fire, so the fire will be extinguished without harming the environment.

Drone Shifts

the system will keep most of the drones hovering over the forest monitoring its status until the other drones get fully charged, so it will guarantee that the forests will be safe and secured 24/7 in summer, as the shift patterns in hospitals.
